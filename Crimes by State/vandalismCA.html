Vandalism, CA



Sentences

  • Maximum Sentence: $500 fine, 10 days in jail
  • Minimum Sentence: $250 fine




Actual law

Simplified

PART 1. OF CRIMES AND PUNISHMENTS [25 - 680] ( Part 1 enacted 1872. ) TITLE 14. MALICIOUS MISCHIEF [594 - 625c] ( Title 14 enacted 1872. )

In California, the crime of vandalism is committed when a person “maliciously” damages or destroys property that he does not own, or where he maliciously defaces property that he does not own with graffiti or other inscribed material.
